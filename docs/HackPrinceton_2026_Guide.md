## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I participate in multiple tracks?**
A: You must choose one "Main Track", but you can enter as many "Special Tracks" as you want!

**Q: How many members per team?**
A: Maximum of 4 members. Solo hackers are also welcome!

**Q: Where can I sleep/rest?**
A: We cannot designate official sleeping areas, but you may rest in classrooms designated as "Hacker Spaces".

**Q: What if I have to leave early?**
A: You are free to leave, but we recommend staying for the Closing Ceremony as you might win a Special Track award!
