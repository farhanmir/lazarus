# Cascade IQ: The Autonomous Enterprise Nervous System

**Cascade IQ** is a high-performance, multi-agent infrastructure designed to transform global supply chain volatility into autonomous, self-healing enterprise operations. While traditional risk management tools are limited to static simulations or passive alerting, Cascade IQ closes the loop—detecting disruptions in real-time, reasoning through complex logistical pivots, and executing multi-step procurement actions.

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture: The Distributed Intelligence Loop

Cascade IQ is engineered as a **distributed state machine** that maintains operational continuity through a multi-agent swarm.

1.  **Detection Layer (The Pulse):** * **Technology:** **Knot API** (`TransactionLink`)
    * **Logic:** Monitors real-time corporate spend and SKU-level data. The system identifies "silent failures"—where a missing expected logistics clearing fee or payment failure signals a port blockage or vendor collapse before it hits the news cycle.

2.  **Reasoning Layer (The Brain):**
    * **Technology:** **IFM K2 Think V2**
    * **Logic:** K2 Think V2 performs high-order strategic reasoning, ingesting the disruption context (e.g., a cyber-strike at a specific terminal) and cross-referencing it with the internal dependency graph. It solves a complex constraint satisfaction problem: balancing **Cost vs. Speed vs. Value-at-Risk (VaR)** to propose an optimal pivot.

3.  **Human-in-the-Loop Gateway (The Interface):**
    * **Technology:** **Photon Spectrum (iMessage)**
    * **Logic:** High-stakes enterprise actions require a cryptographic-style human signature. Cascade IQ pings leadership via iMessage with a concise brief. This "Hybrid Intelligence" ensures the AI remains the pilot, but the human holds the keys.

4.  **Execution Layer (The Limbs):**
    * **Technology:** **Eragon OpenClaw (Browser-Use)**
    * **Logic:** Upon authorization, an autonomous agent executes "Real Work Monday." It navigates legacy internal procurement portals (SAP/Oracle mocks) via browser manipulation—logging in, searching alternative vendors, and drafting a $250k+ Purchase Order for final human verification.

5.  **Persistence Layer (The Shield):**
    * **Technology:** **Dedalus Containers**
    * **Logic:** The entire swarm is containerized to ensure production-grade reliability, fault tolerance, and persistent state across asynchronous execution cycles.

## 🎯 The "Ah-ha" Moment: The Savannah Port Scenario

Our demo simulates a critical infrastructure failure: **"The Savannah Port Cyber-Strike."**

1.  **Scenario:** Corporate credit card data (Knot) shows a sudden halt in clearing fees for a critical microchip SKU.
2.  **Reasoning:** K2 Think V2 analyzes the "Chain of Thought" to suggest a pivot to a domestic distributor. It justifies a 12% price increase as the only way to prevent a $2M assembly line shutdown.
3.  **Action:** The judge sees a text on the CEO's phone. A single **"YES"** reply triggers a live browser window where the robot (OpenClaw) autonomously fills out the complex enterprise procurement form in real-time.
