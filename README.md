# Cascade IQ: The Autonomous Enterprise Nervous System

**Cascade IQ** is an autonomous enterprise orchestrator designed to transform global supply chain disruptions into immediate, self-healing actions. While standard tools merely simulate risk or alert teams, Cascade IQ uses a containerized swarm of agents to monitor real-time chaos—such as port strikes, payment failures, or cyberattacks—and bridge the gap to internal operations.

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture

Cascade IQ operates through a **Chain Reaction of Intelligence**:
1.  **Detection (The Pulse):** Uses **Knot API** (`TransactionLink`) to monitor corporate spend. A missing expected transaction (e.g., a logistics clearing fee) signals a "silent" supply chain failure.
2.  **Strategy (The Brain):** **IFM K2 Think V2** performs deep strategic reasoning. It ingests the disruption context and SKU-level data to solve complex constraint satisfaction problems (Cost vs. Speed vs. Risk).
3.  **Approval (The Interface):** **Photon Spectrum** integrates with **iMessage**. The CEO/COO receives a concise strategic summary and can authorize a million-dollar pivot with a simple "YES" text.
4.  **Action (The Limbs):** **Eragon OpenClaw** (Browser-Use) navigates internal procurement portals (SAP/Oracle mocks) to find alternative vendors, draft Purchase Orders, and prepare them for final execution.
5.  **Infrastructure (The Shield):** Hosted on **Dedalus Containers** to ensure production-grade reliability and agent persistence.
